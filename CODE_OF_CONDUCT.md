# CODE OF CONDUCT

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in NEXUS-SENTRY a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome newcomers and beginners
- Accept constructive criticism positively
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible forclarifying standards of acceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders.
